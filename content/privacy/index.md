## Introduction

ScreenlessTracker ("we", "us", "our") operates the website screenlesstracker.com. This page explains how we handle your information when you visit our site.

## Information We Collect

### Automatically Collected Information

When you visit our site, standard server logs may record:

- Your IP address (anonymised)
- Browser type and version
- Pages visited and time spent
- Referring website

This data is used solely for analytics to understand how our content is used and to improve the site.

### Cookies

We may use cookies to:

- Understand site traffic via analytics tools
- Remember your preferences

You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time. This will not affect your ability to read content on our site.

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

- Analyse site performance and improve our content
- Understand which reviews and guides are most useful
- Ensure the site functions correctly

We do **not** sell, trade or rent your personal information to any third party.

## Third-Party Services

We may use third-party analytics services (such as Google Analytics). These services have their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review them.

Where we include affiliate links to products, clicking these links may place cookies from third-party retailers. We have no control over these cookies.

## Affiliate Disclosure

Some links on ScreenlessTracker may be affiliate links. This means we may earn a small commission if you purchase through these links, at no extra cost to you. Affiliate relationships do not influence our editorial opinions.

## Data Retention

We retain anonymised analytics data for up to 26 months for trend analysis purposes.

## Your Rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to:

- Access the personal data we hold about you
- Request correction or deletion of your data
- Object to or restrict processing of your data

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us.

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The date at the top of this page indicates when it was last revised.
