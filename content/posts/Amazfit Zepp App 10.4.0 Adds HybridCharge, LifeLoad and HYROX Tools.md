If you opened the Zepp app this week and thought, “Hang on, wasn’t this called BioCharge yesterday?”, you are not imagining things. Zepp Health has started rolling out version 10.4.0, and the headline change is that BioCharge is being reworked and rebranded as HybridCharge.[chinesesmartwatches]
At first glance, this looks like standard fitness app housekeeping: new label, same score, move along. But this one is a bit more interesting than that, because Zepp is no longer relying only on watch data like sleep, heart rate and workouts to judge how ready you are for training.[chinesesmartwatches]
What HybridCharge actually does
For ages, the Zepp app used BioCharge as its daily readiness score. It pulled together things your watch already tracks automatically, including sleep duration, heart rate and exercise, then turned that into a rough guide for how prepared your body might be for another session.[chinesesmartwatches]
HybridCharge still does that basic job, but now it adds something wearables usually struggle with: context. Instead of trusting sensor data alone, the app also asks how you actually feel, which is a sensible move when real life has a habit of messing with neat recovery scores.[chinesesmartwatches]
A smartwatch can spot poor sleep or a higher resting heart rate. It cannot tell whether you are stressed from work, fighting off a cold, carrying a headache, or somehow feeling brilliant despite the numbers suggesting otherwise. HybridCharge is Zepp’s attempt to close that gap.[chinesesmartwatches]
The useful new bits
The standout addition is LifeLoad Logs. This lets you record how you feel day to day, including things like sickness, tiredness, feeling energised, sore muscles or aching joints, and each one can be marked as low, medium or high impact. There is also a chat-style option, which looks designed to make the process feel less like admin and more like something you might actually keep using.[chinesesmartwatches]
Zepp has also added Training Focus, which looks back across the previous week of exercise and splits your work into strength and endurance. That is more useful than another single daily score, because it shows whether your training is reasonably balanced or whether you have accidentally spent seven straight days pretending zone two cardio or heavy squats are the entire fitness universe.[chinesesmartwatches]
Then there is the HYROX angle. The update adds a HYROX Training Library with structured workouts, alongside a Race Analysis feature that helps users review performance and spot where they can improve. For anyone training in that run-lift-suffer overlap, this is the part of the update that feels genuinely practical.[chinesesmartwatches]
Still not fully baked
There is one slightly messy bit to all this. While HybridCharge is now appearing inside the phone app, many Amazfit watches are still showing the older BioCharge name on-device, which makes the rollout feel a little out of sync.[chinesesmartwatches]
That does not mean the feature is broken, but it does suggest Zepp Health is pushing changes in stages rather than flipping everything over at once. Long-time Amazfit users will probably see this and think, yes, that sounds about right.[chinesesmartwatches]
The article also notes that the app update is currently available on the iOS App Store and is coming soon to Google Play for Android. So if Android users still cannot see it yet, that is likely down to rollout timing rather than anything more dramatic.[chinesesmartwatches]
Who should pay attention
This update matters most to people who already care about readiness scores and recovery trends, because HybridCharge should be more useful than BioCharge if users actually bother to log how they feel. That last bit matters, because every wearable platform loves manual logging features on launch day, and far fewer people still love them a week later.[chinesesmartwatches]
For runners, gym users and HYROX athletes, the bigger win may actually be Training Focus and the expanded HYROX support rather than the new name. Those features push Zepp a bit closer to being a genuinely helpful training companion instead of just a place where graphs go to live.[chinesesmartwatches]
FAQs
Is HybridCharge just BioCharge with a new name?
Not quite. Zepp has renamed BioCharge to HybridCharge, but it has also changed how the score works by combining watch data with user-entered wellbeing feedback.[chinesesmartwatches]
What is LifeLoad in the Zepp app?
LifeLoad is a new daily logging tool that lets users record things like fatigue, sickness, energy levels, muscle soreness and joint discomfort, each with a low, medium or high impact setting.[chinesesmartwatches]
What does Training Focus do?
Training Focus reviews the previous week of exercise and breaks it into strength and endurance categories, helping users see whether their training mix is balanced.[chinesesmartwatches]
Does the update include HYROX features?
Yes. Zepp app 10.4.0 adds a HYROX Training Library and a Race Analysis feature aimed at users training for that style of competition.[chinesesmartwatches]
Why does my watch still say BioCharge?
Because the rollout appears to be incomplete for now. The phone app is showing HybridCharge, while some watches are still displaying the older BioCharge branding.[chinesesmartwatches]
